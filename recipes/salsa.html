Salsa

Ingredients

  • 14 oz Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 bunch Green Onions
  • 2-3 Jalepeno (or other pepper)
  • 1 bunch Cilantro
  • 3-5 Cloves of Garlic
  • Lime (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Tortilla Chips, for serving

    • Preperation

  • Chop green onion, jalapeno, cilantro, and garlic into large chunks
  • Combine all ingredients in a large blender (or work in multiple batches)
  • Blend together intil is it a slightly chunky consistency
  • Taste, add more salt or lime if needed, and serve

    • Optional

    Before blending, use a smoking cloche on ingredients to add a nice smokey flavor
